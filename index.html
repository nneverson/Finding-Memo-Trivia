This trivia game consists of 10 multiple choice questions. You will be tested on your knowledge of the 2003 movie "Finding Nemo" as well as marine biology.

For expample, on a question the correct answer is 1 but 2, 3, 4, were selected before 1, the player has lost 30 points. There is no way to gain points, only to lose points.
